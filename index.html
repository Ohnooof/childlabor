Who We Are

Weyland Co is a organization set on revealing the dark side of child labor.

Heart

Compassion is the driving force behind social change. Understanding the human side of child labor—how it affects children emotionally, mentally, and physically—inspires deeper feelings and a commitment to action.

Hope

We must look forward to the future regarding child labor, and have hope that these abused children are able to be freed from their pain. Without this hope, attempting to inhibit and eradicate child labor would become an extremely exhausting task.

Heroism

Weyland, the founder of this movement, is a hero himself. He goes to abused kids and helps them break free from their shackled. Anyone can be a hero, even you. It just starts with doing the right thing, such as raising awareness about what these poor kids are going through.

Humanity

Child labor undermines basic human rights, depriving children of their childhood, education, and potential. Humanity calls for empathy, justice, and respect for all individuals, especially the most vulnerable.

Problem & Solutions

Children are forced to work because of a variety of reasons, ranging from financial instability of the family to forceful enslavement.

We have created three potential solutions to progress in ending child labor

Spark Courage

Sparking courage and confidence in the victims themselves can help them stand up for their rights as human beings. It is with the cooperation of the victims that we will be able to bring any major change into the pitch dark side of child labor.

Send Spies

Through cooperation with law enforcement around the world, we would be able to send spies to infiltrate corrupt organizations and hopefully free many kids who are subjected to child labor.

Raising Awareness

Through raising awareness we can show the entire world the horrors of child labor. We can garner the attention of governments and force them to take action, such as improving the wage of parents so as to give financial stability to the family, as well as many other ways. The change starts with you.
